Summary

Only i know the end of this World.

One day our MC Kim Dokja finds himself stuck in the world of his favorite webnovel. What does he to do survive? It is a world struck by casastrophe and danger all around.

His edge? He knows the plot of the story to end. Because he was the solo reader that stuck with it. Read the story to see how he survives!
